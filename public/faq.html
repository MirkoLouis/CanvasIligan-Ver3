Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Using CanvasIligan is simple. Just enter what you're looking for in the search bar on the main page and press "Search". You can search for a product by its name (e.g., "Arduino Uno"), its purpose (e.g., "microcontroller for a school project"), or a general description (e.g., "a small board to control LEDs"). The system will find the most relevant products available in local stores. You can also filter results by category and navigate through pages using the controls at the bottom.

CanvasIligan is a specialized search engine designed to help you find electronic products and components within Iligan City. Unlike traditional search engines that just match keywords, CanvasIligan uses artificial intelligence to understand the *meaning* of your search. This allows it to provide more accurate and relevant results, helping you discover the right products for your projects, even if you don't know the exact terminology.

Our system uses a technology called "semantic search." Here's a simple breakdown:
  1. Indexing: We first analyze the name and description of every product from our partner stores and convert them into numerical representations called "vector embeddings." These vectors capture the product's meaning.
  2. Querying: When you enter a search query, we convert your text into a similar vector.
  3. Matching: The system then rapidly searches for the product vectors that are closest in meaning to your query vector.
This process allows us to find "vibration motor" when you search for "a small thing that shakes," because the meanings are semantically similar.
